About Us

Size Label International was founded in 2011. We are a company engaged in the printing of labels, stickers, and accessories for the garment industry. Contact us Now

Our services start from designing, making samples, producing, controlling quality, and packaging until delivery to customers. To produce quality products, we cannot be separated from the process of monitoring and testing products to provide the best service for customers. We have served many customers who are engaged in manufacturing shoes, dolls, towels, clothes, and many more.

Our printing process is also equipped with modern machines and equipment with experienced personnel. Over time we continue to innovate to improve service quality and provide the best solutions according to customer needs.
